Prometheux can be deployed in multiple ways depending on your infrastructure
and requirements. Choose the option that best fits your environment.

| Method | Best For | Infrastructure | Effort |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [**Prometheux Cloud**](/get-started/installation/cloud) | Fastest start, no infra to manage | None (hosted by Prometheux) | Minimal |
| [**On (Cloud) Premises**](/get-started/installation/on-premises/overview) | Own VM or cluster on AWS, Azure, GCP, or bare metal | Docker / Yarn / Kubernetes | Low–High |
| [**Snowflake Native App**](/get-started/installation/snowflake) | Teams already on Snowflake | Snowflake account | Low |
| [**Databricks**](/get-started/installation/databricks/overview) | Teams already on Databricks | Databricks workspace | Medium |
